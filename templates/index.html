Gender Pay Gap Analysis!


In this project we set out to analyze the different factors contributing towards the pay of a persron(education, age, gender etc). Importantly, this analysis focuses on the gender pay gap(if any) that exists. We have tried to look at our data from varying angles and extract some really useful information. Here, we use the Kaggler's dataset, where several kagglers were asked a variety of questions pertaining to their profession, country, age, education, salary range and a lot more.
